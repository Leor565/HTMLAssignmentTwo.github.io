Issues With Protected Areas


Introduction:

Protected areas throughout the world perform many functions. They are essential for conserving biodiversity, and for delivering vital ecosystem services, such as protecting watersheds and soils and shielding human communities from natural disasters. Many protected areas are important to local communities. Protected areas are chosen by the government or by local communities such as cities and or local government.
